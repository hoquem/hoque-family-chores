# Privacy Policy — Hoque Family Chores

_Last updated: 6 July 2026_

Hoque Family Chores ("the App") is a private family chore-management application. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

## Information We Collect

- **Account information.** When you create an account we collect your email address and display name, used solely for signing in and identifying you to other members of your own family group.
- **Family and chore data.** Family group names, chore/task details, task assignments, completion status, and points are stored so the App can function.
- **Diagnostics.** The App uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymized crash reports and basic device information (device model, OS version) to help fix bugs.

## How Information Is Used

Your information is used only to:

- Provide the App's core functionality (managing chores within your family group)
- Maintain your account and family membership
- Diagnose and fix crashes and errors

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing. There is no advertising in the App.

## Data Storage

Data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication). Access to family data is restricted to authenticated members of the same family group by server-side security rules.

## Children's Privacy

The App is designed for use by families, with accounts created and managed by parents or guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. Child accounts contain only a display name and the chore activity entered within the family group. Parents may request deletion of a child's data at any time (see Contact below).

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the address below; deletion requests are honored within 30 days.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
